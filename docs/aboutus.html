AMREF Flying Doctors: Lifesaving Care from the Skies

AMREF Flying Doctors is Africa's premier provider of fixed-wing air ambulance services, delivering critical medical evacuation and healthcare solutions across the continent and beyond. Based at Wilson Airport in Nairobi, Kenya, this renowned organization has been a beacon of hope for over 65 years, bridging the gap between remote communities and advanced medical care. As a vital arm of Amref Health Africa, the largest health development non-governmental organization on the continent, AMREF Flying Doctors combines cutting-edge aviation technology with world-class medical expertise to save lives in even the most challenging environments.

OUR MISSION

At the heart of AMREF Flying Doctors' operations is a commitment to providing rapid, reliable, and compassionate aeromedical services.

We aim to ensure that no one is denied access to life-saving care due to distance, terrain, or circumstance. As a social enterprise, any surplus generated from our operations is reinvested into Amref Health Africa's humanitarian initiatives, supporting sustainable health improvements across Africa.

Impact and Commitment

Every mission reflects our core values: excellence, humanity, and unrivaled care. Whether stabilizing a critically ill patient mid-flight or training the next generation of emergency responders, AMREF Flying Doctors is more than an air ambulance service—we are a lifeline. By supporting Amref Health Africa’s broader mission, we contribute to lasting health change, ensuring that quality care reaches even the farthest corners of the continent.
